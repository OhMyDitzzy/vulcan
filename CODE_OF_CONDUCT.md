# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone interested in contributing to Vulcan.

We pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment‑free experience for all.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and considerate
- Using inclusive and welcoming language
- Giving constructive feedback
- Focusing on what's best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Insults, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others’ private information
- Disruptive or hostile conduct

## Our Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for:
- Clarifying acceptable behavior
- Taking appropriate corrective action
- Removing or rejecting harmful contributions

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to:
- The project repository
- Issue trackers
- Pull request discussions
- Community spaces related to Vulcan

## Enforcement
Violations may result in:
- Warnings
- Temporary or permanent bans
- Removal from the community

To report violations, contact the project maintainer privately.
